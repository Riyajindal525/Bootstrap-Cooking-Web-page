Fruit Salad Recipe

Ingredients: Apple, Banana, Orange, Grapes, Honey, Lemon juice.

Method:

  1. Chop all fruits into small pieces.
  2. Mix honey and lemon juice to prepare the dressing.
  3. Toss all chopped fruits well with the dressing.
  4. Serve chilled.

Vegetable Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients: Bread, Tomato, Cucumber, Lettuce, Cheese, Mayonnaise.

Method:

  1. Toast the bread slices.
  2. Spread mayonnaise on the bread.
  3. Add sliced tomato, cucumber, lettuce, and cheese.
  4. Assemble the sandwich and serve.

Chocolate Milkshake Recipe

Ingredients: Milk, Chocolate syrup, Ice cream, Whipped cream.

Method:

  1. Pour milk into a blender.
  2. Add chocolate syrup and ice cream.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Pour into a glass and top with whipped cream.

5-Minute Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients: Mixed fruits, yogurt, honey, ice cubes.

Method:

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Serve chilled.

Microwave Mug Cake Recipe

Ingredients: Flour, sugar, cocoa powder, milk, oil.

Method:

  1. Mix all dry ingredients in a mug.
  2. Add milk and oil, stir well.
  3. Microwave for 2 minutes.
  4. Enjoy warm.

Quick Garlic Pasta Recipe

Ingredients: Pasta, garlic, olive oil, parmesan, herbs.

Method:

  1. Boil pasta until al dente.
  2. Sauté garlic in olive oil.
  3. Toss pasta with garlic oil, parmesan, and herbs.
  4. Serve hot.

Health & Nutrition Corner

Benefits of Eating Berries

Packed with antioxidants, berries are great for your heart.

Protein in Chickpeas

Chickpeas are a great plant-based protein source.

Detailed Health & Nutrition Info

1. Antioxidants in Berries: Berries like blueberries, raspberries, and blackcurrants are rich in antioxidants that help reduce oxidative stress, improve heart health, and support the immune system.

2. Protein in Chickpeas: Chickpeas provide a rich source of plant-based protein, fiber, and essential nutrients that help in muscle repair, digestion, and maintaining energy levels.

3. Vitamins & Minerals: Both berries and chickpeas contain important vitamins such as vitamin C, vitamin K, and minerals like iron, magnesium, and potassium that support overall wellness.

4. Dietary Fiber: The high fiber content in these foods helps regulate blood sugar, promotes satiety, and supports digestive health.

5. Anti-inflammatory Properties: Regular consumption may reduce inflammation and lower risk of chronic diseases.

Grandma’s Special Curry Recipe

Ingredients: Mixed vegetables, spices, coconut milk, garlic, ginger.

Method:

  1. Sauté garlic and ginger in oil.
  2. Add mixed vegetables and spices.
  3. Pour coconut milk and simmer.
  4. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  5. Serve hot with rice or bread.

Chocolate Fudge Brownies Recipe

Ingredients: Dark chocolate, butter, sugar, eggs, flour, cocoa powder.

Method:

  1. Melt chocolate and butter together.
  2. Whisk sugar and eggs until fluffy.
  3. Combine with chocolate mixture.
  4. Add flour and cocoa powder.
  5. Bake at 180°C for 25 minutes.
  6. Cool, cut, and serve.

Vegan Buddha Bowl Recipe

Ingredients: Quinoa, Chickpeas, Avocado, Mixed veggies, Tahini sauce.

Method:

  1. Cook quinoa and let it cool.
  2. Roast chickpeas with spices.
  3. Chop avocado and veggies.
  4. Mix everything and drizzle tahini sauce.
  5. Serve fresh.

Avocado Toast Recipe

Ingredients: Bread, Avocado, Lemon juice, Chili flakes, Salt, Pepper.

Method:

  1. Toast the bread slices.
  2. Mash avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the mixture on toast.
  4. Sprinkle chili flakes on top.
  5. Serve immediately.

Blueberry Pancakes Recipe

Ingredients: Flour, Milk, Eggs, Blueberries, Baking powder, Sugar.

Method:

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, and sugar.
  2. Whisk milk and eggs, combine with dry mix.
  3. Fold in blueberries.
  4. Cook on hot griddle until golden.
  5. Serve with syrup or honey.

Chocolate Lava Cake Recipe

Ingredients: Dark chocolate, Butter, Eggs, Sugar, Flour.

Method:

  1. Melt chocolate and butter together.
  2. Whisk eggs and sugar until fluffy.
  3. Combine chocolate mixture with eggs.
  4. Add flour gently.
  5. Bake at 220°C for 12 mins.
  6. Serve warm with ice cream.
